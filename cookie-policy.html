Cookie notice

How this website may use cookies and preference storage.

This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, which types may be used on this website, how consent choices are stored, and how visitors can manage cookie preferences.

1. What cookies are

Cookies are small text files that may be stored on your device when you visit a website. They can help a website work correctly, remember preferences, improve usability, or provide basic information about website performance.

Similar technologies, such as local storage, may also be used to remember choices made by visitors, including cookie banner preferences.

2. How Union s.r.o. may use cookies

Union s.r.o. may use cookies or local storage to support website functionality, remember consent preferences, improve user experience, and understand general website performance if analytics tools are implemented.

This website does not require visitors to create an account to browse pages. Cookies are not intended to collect payment information or sensitive identity data.

3. Essential cookies

Essential cookies or local storage items help the website function properly. These may be used for basic technical operation, security, accessibility, cookie preference storage, and smooth website behavior.

  • Remembering whether a visitor accepted or declined the cookie banner.
  • Supporting basic website interface behavior.
  • Maintaining security and stability of the website.

Essential cookies are usually necessary for the website to work and may not require separate consent depending on applicable law.

4. Analytics or performance cookies

Analytics or performance cookies may be used if the site owner implements analytics tools. These tools can help understand general website usage, such as which pages are visited, how users navigate, and where improvements may be needed.

Analytics information should generally be used in an aggregated or limited way to improve website clarity and performance. If analytics tools are added, they may be provided by third-party platforms with their own privacy and cookie practices.

5. Preference cookies

Preference cookies or local storage may be used to remember choices you make on the website, such as cookie banner decisions or interface preferences.

For example, the cookie banner may store whether you selected “Accept” or “Decline” so the banner does not appear again on every page.

6. Marketing cookies

Marketing cookies are typically used to support advertising, retargeting, or campaign measurement. This website should not use marketing cookies unless such tools are intentionally added by the site owner and disclosed where required.

If advertising or retargeting tools are added in the future, this Cookie Policy should be updated to explain how those tools work and how visitors can manage related choices.

7. Cookie consent

This website includes a cookie banner that allows visitors to accept or decline cookie use where applicable. The banner does not close automatically and stores a visitor’s choice after selection.

  • Accept stores an accepted preference.
  • Decline stores a declined preference.
  • The banner should not appear again after a choice is saved.
Cookie banner storage The website may use localStorage to remember cookie banner choices. In the current configuration, the storage key is managed through the website config.

8. Third-party tools

This website may use third-party tools for fonts, icons, hosting, analytics, forms, or website functionality. These third parties may process technical information according to their own terms and privacy practices.

If additional third-party tools are installed, the site owner should review their cookie behavior and update this policy where necessary.

9. How to manage cookies

You can manage cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to block cookies, delete existing cookies, or receive alerts before cookies are stored.

Disabling some cookies may affect website functionality or prevent preferences from being remembered.

10. How to change consent

If you want to change your cookie choice, you can clear this website’s cookies and local storage in your browser settings. After clearing stored preferences, the cookie banner may appear again.

If the website later includes a dedicated cookie preferences panel, you may use that panel to update your choice more directly.

11. Relationship with the Privacy Policy

Cookies and similar technologies may involve the processing of limited personal or technical information. For more information about how Union s.r.o. may handle website information, review the Privacy Policy.
